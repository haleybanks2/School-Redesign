Welcome To A Better Education

As of the 2016/2017 school-year Hazard High School was ranked 17th in the state of Kentucky. Our faculty solely focus on the bettering of our students' education. We bulldogs take pride in our work ethic and sports. This past year our football team advanced to the state championship, and our speech and academic teams both advanced to state.
